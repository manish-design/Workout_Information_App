back bicep BARBEL PREACHER CURL

Body Part: BICEP

Description:

1) Placena EZ-bar on the rack of preacher bench and load with appropriate weight.

2) Adjust height of seating and bench until the bench reaches comfirtable
position under accross chest and under the armpits, while allowing full flexion and extension of the upper arms and forearms.

3) Grasp bar with both hand and contract bicep to bring the bar towards the face.

4) Once at the full contraction, with controlled motions reversed the bar's direction and the back until the back biceps are extended to complete the repetition.

Sets and Reps: 12-10-8-6

Weight: 5 kg/lbs

 DUMBEELL CONCENTRATION CURL

Body Part: BICEP

Description:

Preparation: sit on bench, grasp
dumbbell between feet. place back
of upper arm to inner thigh. lean
into leg to raise elbows lightly.

Execution: Raise dumbbell to front
of shoulder. lower dumbbell until
arm is fully extended. Repeate. continue with opposite arm.

Sets and Reps: 12-10-8-6

Weight: 5 kg/lbs
