shoulder Barbell Front Raise

Body Part: Shoulder

Description:

1) Set the barbell by loading a straight bar or a
EZ bar with weight you want to use.

2) Grasp the bar with an overhand grip (palms facing down)
with your hands around shoulder width apart.
3) stand straight up and let the barbell rest
on your thighs.
4) Straighten your back, tnese your mid section and pull
your shoulder back, taking the weight off your
thighs and holding it about 5 inches from your body
this the starting position of the exercise.
5) keeping ypur arms straight slowly Raise the barbell
up to the shoulder height.
6) Pause, and then slowly lower the barbell back to
the starting position

Sets and Reps: 12-10-8-6

Weight: 5 kg/lbs

 Dumbbell Front Raises

Body Part: Shoulder

Description:

Grasp Dumbell in both hands, Position Dumbells
in front of upper legs with elbows straight
or slightly bent. Raise Dumbell forward and
upward until upper arms are above horizontal.
Lower and Repeat.

Sets and Reps: 12-10-8-6

Weight: 5 kg/lbs

 Seated Front Press

Body Part: Shoulder

Description:

1) Sit on a bench with back support in a
squat rack. position a barbell at
a height that is just above your head
2) Grab THE Barbell with pronated grip
(palms facing forward). once you pick
the barbell with correct grip width, lift
the bar up over your head by your arms.
hold at about shoulder level and slightly
in front of your. this is your starting
position.
3) lower the bar down to the shoulder slowly
as you inhale. lift the bar back to the
starting position as you exhale.

Sets and Reps: 12-10-8-6

Weight: 5 kg/lbs
